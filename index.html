Join us on September 28, 2024, at Makukhanye Art Room Khayelitsha, Cape Town, for the Ugayi Poetry Festival. Experience an enriching day celebrating African heritage through powerful poetry performances and community engagement.

About Us

Founded by Dinileminyanya(Okuhle Ntloyiya), Ugayi Poetry Festival is dedicated to celebrating African culture and poetry. Our goal is to support local poets, foster community engagement, and promote cultural pride.

Okuhle Ntloyiya is an acclaimed poet with a passion for reviving and celebrating African heritage through poetry.

Celebrating Our First Annual Event: 19 June 2022

The journey of UGAYI began with a dream, and on 19 June 2022, that dream became a reality. Our first annual event was both exhilarating and daunting, especially for Okuhle Ntloyiya, who had never hosted such a significant show before. The lineup started small, but within just two weeks, poets from across the provinces, particularly the Eastern Cape, expressed their eagerness to participate. Okuhle's mix of happiness and fear transformed into a spectacular event that laid the foundation for UGAYI's future. Read more

UGAYI Birthday Celebration: 19 June 2023

On 19 June 2023, UGAYI celebrated its first birthday in grand style. The event was so immense it felt more like a festival than a birthday celebration. The community's support and the growing interest from poets and attendees alike made this celebration a remarkable success. We look back with pride and gratitude, and forward with excitement for what lies ahead. Read more

Taking UGAYI to the Eastern Cape: 23 September 2023

On 23 September 2023, UGAYI ventured to the Eastern Cape, specifically to the rural area of Qamata in Cofimvaba. The warm welcome and love we received from the local community were overwhelming. This event not only highlighted the rich cultural heritage of the area but also strengthened our commitment to bringing poetry and artistic expression to all corners of South Africa. Read more

UGAYI Festival 2024: A Grand Celebration in Cape Town

Mark your calendars for 28 September 2024, as UGAYI gears up for its biggest festival yet! With poets from Gauteng, Eastern Cape, Western Cape, Free State, and KwaZulu-Natal set to perform, this year's event promises to be a monumental celebration of art and culture.
